MASERATI

Maserati, an Italian luxury car manufacturer founded in Bologna in 1914, has established itself as a symbol of elegance and performance. Recognized for its sleek designs and distinctive trident logo, Maserati has a storied history of crafting high-end sports cars, sedans, and SUVs. The brand's racing heritage, including victories at prestigious events like the Indianapolis 500, has contributed to its allure. Maserati's commitment to craftsmanship is evident in models such as the Quattroporte, Ghibli, and Levante, each blending style and performance. The trident emblem, inspired by the statue of Neptune in Bologna's Piazza Maggiore, symbolizes strength and vigor.
