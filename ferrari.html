FERRARI

Ferrari, the renowned Italian luxury sports car manufacturer founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939, stands as a symbol of automotive excellence and passion. With a rich racing heritage, including the iconic Scuderia Ferrari Formula One team, the brand has consistently pushed the boundaries of performance and innovation. Ferrari's distinct prancing horse logo, inspired by a World War I fighter plane, reflects its commitment to speed and success. The company has produced legendary models like the Ferrari 250 GTO and F40, blending cutting-edge technology with exquisite craftsmanship. Known for limited production runs, owning a Ferrari is a symbol of exclusivity.
